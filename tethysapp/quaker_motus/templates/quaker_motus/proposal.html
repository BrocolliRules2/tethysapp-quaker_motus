Motus Proposal

This application is designed to help calculate ground motion acceleration due to Earthquakes in the Western United states. To do so, click anywhere on the map and a pop up menu will appear showing the ground motion acceleration, as well as the 80% and 50% ground acceleration.

Click on the next tab to get started.
